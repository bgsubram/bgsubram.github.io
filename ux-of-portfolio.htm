Accenture library remodeling

Note: Due to Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA)- no pictures of the existing portal can be shared

01 concept | brainstorm | information gathering

​

Accenture library is a knowledge powerhouse for employees to improve their skillset, vocabulary, knowledge or even read to relax. Many useful resources are present in the library portal and the employees can access the online resources at any time as long as they are employed. Since many of the employees were unaware of the existence of the library within the company, we decided on spreading awareness and re-modelling the library portal, creating a mobile app for the frequently used URLs.

​

Initial ideation:

  • Firstly we had to make the employees aware of the existence of the library

  • Our users would be the internal employees of the company

    • Since we had the mailing list handy – newsletters would be a great way to spread awareness

    • Posters within the company to see to that employees can access the information

    • Organizing team events and contests in the library or through newsletters

My role during this phase: (1st Sprint)

  • Designing the posters with the company's branding to be printed out and displayed in all accessible assembling areas of the employees like cafeteria, break-out area, meeting rooms etc.,

  • Responsible to design the newsletter template and share across to the project manager who would be sending throughout the cities employee mailing list

  • I also designed contest circulars for Library events

​

02 user research

​

  • The posters were printed and displayed across the company. 

  • I prepared the newsletter template and it was shared across every Monday - called motivational Mondays

  • There were several contests going through mail like crosswords etc., and the library portal was now getting some hits and usage

  • Here is when our second sprint started
